About TEKhasa

TEKhasa is Kawing Innovations' training arm and focuses on the convergence of technology and community development through education. Through TEKhasa, we aim to equip Filipinos with skills needed in navigating the 21st century.

We believe that education and technology can bring benefit to communities and to the lives of Filipinos, especially if they have access. Through training and competency-building of future technology, education and community experts, we are making a contribution to narrowing the digital divide and in the uplifting our society.

TEKhasa is a combination of the following words: Teknolohiya + Edukasyon + Komunidad + Hasa (Filipino word which means to "sharpen").
